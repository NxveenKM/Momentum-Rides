5 Tips for Renting the Perfect Car for a Rajasthan Wedding

Published on August 22, 2025 by Vikram Choudhary

Planning a wedding in the land of kings? Congratulations! A Rajasthan wedding is an unforgettable experience, and transportation plays a key role in its success. Choosing the right rental cars ensures comfort for your guests and a grand entrance for the couple. Here are our top five tips.

1. Book Well in Advance

Jaipur is a global wedding hotspot, especially during peak season (October to March). To ensure you get the cars you want, especially luxury or vintage models, it's crucial to book at least 3-6 months in advance. This also helps in securing better rates.

2. Choose the Right Vehicle Mix

Think about your different needs. You'll likely require a mix of vehicles: a luxury sedan like a Ford Mustang for the couple's grand entrance, comfortable SUVs like the Kia Seltos for family, and spacious vans like the Toyota Innova for guest airport transfers.

3. Opt for a Chauffeur Service

Don't add the stress of driving and parking to your family's plate. Our professional chauffeur services ensure that everyone arrives at the venue on time and relaxed. Our drivers are locals who know the city's routes and traffic patterns inside out.

4. Consider Decorations

Ask your rental provider about their policies on car decorations. At Momentum Rides, we work with top wedding planners and florists in Jaipur to ensure your wedding car is decorated elegantly to match your theme, right from the bonnet to the boot.

5. Confirm All-Inclusive Pricing

To avoid any last-minute surprises, confirm that your rental package is all-inclusive. This should cover the driver's fee, fuel, tolls, and any state taxes. A transparent, upfront cost will help you manage your wedding budget effectively.

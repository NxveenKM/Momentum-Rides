Our Story

Founded in the vibrant city of Jaipur, Momentum Rides was born from a passion for travel and a desire to make exploring the incredible landscapes of Rajasthan simple, safe, and unforgettable. Our founder, a Jaipur native and avid traveler, noticed a gap for a car rental service that offered not just vehicles, but genuine local expertise and a commitment to transparent, customer-first service. We started with just three cars and a simple mission: to be the most trusted name in travel for every visitor to our beautiful state.

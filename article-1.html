Top 5 Weekend Road Trips from Jaipur

Published on August 21, 2025 by Naveen Kumar Meena

Escape the city and discover the magic of Rajasthan. From the holy city of Pushkar to the majestic forts of Alwar, here are five unforgettable weekend getaways just a drive away from Jaipur. All you need is a full tank of fuel and a thirst for adventure!

1. Pushkar: The Spiritual Oasis

Distance from Jaipur: ~145 km

A drive to Pushkar is a journey into the spiritual heart of Rajasthan. Famous for its sacred lake, vibrant ghats, and the only Brahma temple in the world, Pushkar offers a serene escape. The drive itself is scenic, taking you through the Aravalli Hills. We recommend a comfortable sedan like a Honda City for this trip.

2. Alwar & Sariska Tiger Reserve

Distance from Jaipur: ~165 km

For wildlife enthusiasts and history buffs, the road to Alwar is a perfect choice. Explore the majestic Bala Qila fort and then head into the wilderness of Sariska Tiger Reserve. An SUV like a Mahindra Thar is the perfect companion for navigating the reserve's rugged terrain.

3. Shekhawati: The Open-Air Art Gallery

Distance from Jaipur: ~115 km to Nawalgarh

The Shekhawati region is famous for its stunning havelis adorned with intricate frescoes. A drive through towns like Mandawa and Nawalgarh feels like stepping back in time. It's a photographer's paradise and a testament to the artistic heritage of Rajasthan.

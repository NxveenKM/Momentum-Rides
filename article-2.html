A Food Lover's Guide to Jaipur's Best Highway Dhabas

Published on August 22, 2025 by Priya Sharma

In Rajasthan, the journey is just as important as the destination, especially when there's delicious food involved. A road trip out of Jaipur isn't complete without a stop at a traditional highway dhaba. We've mapped out the best spots for an authentic culinary experience.

1. Sharma Dhaba (Jaipur-Agra Highway, NH21)

A legendary spot famous for its authentic Dal Baati Churma. Cooked over a traditional fire, the flavors are rich and smoky. It's a must-visit for anyone heading towards Agra or beyond. Don't forget to try their masala chai!

2. Raja Park Dhaba (Jaipur-Delhi Highway, NH48)

Known for its incredible variety of parathas, this dhaba is a favorite among truckers and families alike. The gobi and aloo-pyaaz parathas, served with a dollop of white butter, are the perfect fuel for a long drive. An SUV like the Kia Seltos gives you a comfortable ride to this popular spot.

3. The Old Rao Dhaba (Jaipur-Ajmer Highway, NH48)

If you're craving authentic Rajasthani non-vegetarian dishes, this is your stop. Their Laal Maas is legendary, and the Ker Sangri is a fantastic vegetarian option. It's a no-frills establishment that focuses purely on incredible taste.
